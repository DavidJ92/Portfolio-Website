About Me

Embarking on my coding journey through online courses, including those from the University of Wisconsin, my journey into development has been a blend of skills, personal growth, and sheer determination. From the shores of Puerto Rico, I've embraced coding not just as a career, but as a canvas for creativity and problem-solving.

Fueled by a passion for challenge and a knack for taking responsibility, I've found coding to be the perfect outlet for my ambitions. Each line of code represents an opportunity to innovate, to push boundaries, and to make a difference.

If you're looking for a developer who brings more than just technical expertise to the table, someone who sees coding as both an art and a science, then I'm your match. Let's collaborate and bring your ideas to life in ways you never thought possible.
